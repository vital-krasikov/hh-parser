Search parameters

Enter the parameters (such as the vacancy or the company name) into the text field and press the Search button.

For the reduction of the execution time the number of vacancies will be bounded by the max_vacancies parameter (integer from 0 to 2000). min_skills_freq parameter (float from 0 to 1) allows to output only the skills which are present more than in the given percent of vacancies.
